# Product Requirements Document (Client-Friendly Version)

**Project Name:** AI Meeting Notes Manager  
**Target Audience:** Client Executive Team & Operations Managers  
**Document Status:** Pending Approval  

---

## 1. Product Purpose & Business Value
The **AI Meeting Notes Manager** converts unstructured spoken meetings and uploaded records into structured, actionable items. Standard note-taking is slow, subjective, and prone to errors. This product standardizes note capture, ensuring absolute clarity on task ownership and project progress while saving teams valuable administrative hours daily.

---

## 2. Key Features & Business Benefits

### A. Centralized Management Dashboard
* **Business Value:** Provides team leaders with a single-pane viewport to track meetings, telemetry metrics, and task completions.
* **Key Visuals:** A sleek visual interface containing storage meters, time-saved trend curves, and completion dials so leadership can quantify efficiency gains at a glance.
* **Productivity Gain:** Removes the need to manually compile progress sheets; statistics auto-update as meetings are logged.

### B. Live Meeting Capture
* **Business Value:** Eliminates manual minutes-taking during active discussions.
* **How it Works:** Users click a single button to initiate in-browser recording. As the team speaks, the system automatically builds the timeline transcript and highlights detected decisions and task allocations in real time.
* **Collaboration Boost:** Allows meeting participants to focus 100% on creative brainstorming rather than writing notes.

### C. Smart File Uploader
* **Business Value:** Converts past records or external recordings into structured summaries.
* **How it Works:** Drag-and-drop text reports (`.txt`), Word documents (`.docx`), PDF briefings, or audio recording clips (`.mp3`, `.wav`) directly into the browser. The system runs an automated processing pipeline to generate summaries.
* **Time Savings:** Retroactively extracts value from meetings that were missed or recorded offline.

### D. Continuous Offline Service Protection (Local Fallback)
* **Business Value:** Guarantees business continuity. If the primary cloud processing servers go offline, the tool automatically degrades to a secure local summarization engine without throwing errors or locking you out of your files.
* **Productivity Gain:** Zero downtime. Teams can keep writing notes and reading historic archives without interruption.

### E. Team Role Access & Security (RBAC)
* **Business Value:** Safeguards company information integrity.
* **How it Works:** Standard team members can search, record, and view archives. Sensitive policy-level configurations (such as data retention durations and compliance audit logs) are restricted, requiring administrator permission.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Prevents accidental deletion of records and unauthorized modification of storage parameters.

---

## 3. User Experience & Accessibility
* **No Tech Training Required:** The interface uses familiar elements (e.g. sidebar navigation, drag-and-drop boxes, and toggle buttons) that require zero learning curve.
* **Google Material Icons:** High-quality icons clearly signpost every action, replacing confusing emojis.
* **Clear Error Warnings:** Friendly prompt notifications pop up to warn users about file-size limits or browser permissions without throwing technical stack traces.
