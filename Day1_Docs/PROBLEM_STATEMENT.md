# PROBLEM STATEMENT

## 1. Current Industry Problems
- Meeting outputs are fragmented across email, chat, documents, and conferencing platforms.
- Manual note-taking is inconsistent, error-prone, and time-consuming.
- Organizations lack a consistent source of truth for decisions and actions.

## 2. Why Meeting Notes Are Difficult to Manage
- Multiple input formats (audio, text, documents) and inconsistent structure.
- Ownership ambiguity: attendees assume someone else will take notes.
- Latency: delayed summaries lose action momentum.
- Searchability: notes distributed across tools are hard to retrieve.

## 3. Problems with Manual Documentation
- Cognitive load reduces meeting participation.
- Quality varies by note-taker; bias and omission are common.
- Manual process delays follow-up and reduces accountability.

## 4. Issues in Remote Collaboration
- Asynchronous participation and time-zone differences hinder alignment.
- Audio-only meetings lack non-verbal cues, decreasing clarity.
- Fragmented storage across drives and chat channels complicate discovery.

## 5. Pain Points by Segment

### Professionals
- PMs: lack of consolidated decision trail; ambiguous responsibilities.
- Executives: need concise recaps for fast decisions.
- Consultants: struggle to standardize outputs across clients.

### Students
- Disorganized lecture notes and poor revision materials.
- Difficulty coordinating group assignments and tracking tasks.

### Startups
- Rapid decision cycles with little documentation.
- Small teams lack bandwidth for administrative follow-up.

## 6. Business Impact
- Lost productivity, missed deadlines, duplicated work.
- Compliance & audit risks in regulated environments.
- Competitive disadvantage due to slower knowledge transfer.

## 7. Product Opportunity
- Automate extraction of summaries, action items, and decisions.
- Provide a single, searchable source of truth with auditability.
- Blend AI generative capabilities with deterministic fallback.

## 8. Why AI Is Useful
- AI interprets context and generates human-readable summaries.
- Extracts entities, owners, deadlines, and decisions more accurately than heuristics.
- Reduces user effort and time-to-action.

## 9. Why a Fallback System Is Necessary
- AI outages, costs, and privacy restrictions can limit generative AI usage.
- Deterministic extractive NLP ensures continuity and predictable outputs.
- The hybrid approach builds trust and supports compliance.

## 10. Success Vision
- Users receive reliable summaries, actions, and decision logs within minutes of uploading content.
- Teams see improved follow-through and better knowledge retention.
- The platform meets enterprise governance needs via retention, audit logging, and data controls.

(End of Problem Statement)
