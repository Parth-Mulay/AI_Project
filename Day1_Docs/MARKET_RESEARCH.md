# MARKET RESEARCH

## 1. Industry Overview
- Category: Meeting productivity and knowledge management.
- Subcomponents: transcription, summarization, searchable archives, integrations.

## 2. Market Size & Opportunity
- Estimated TAM (2026): $6–8B for meeting and collaboration productivity tools.
- SAM (AI-enhanced notes): $1.2–2.1B focused on teams and knowledge workers.

## 3. Growth Trends
- AI adoption accelerating across enterprise apps since 2023.
- Hybrid work increases demand for asynchronous collaboration tools.

## 4. Remote Work Statistics
- Remote/hybrid workforce ~40–60% in mature markets.
- Average meetings per worker: 6–12 per week; significant time spent on prep and follow-up.

## 5. Hybrid Work Trends
- Asynchronous collaboration tools are high priority.
- Need for searchable meeting archives and decision provenance is growing.

## 6. AI Adoption
- Generative AI adoption rising; organizations concerned about hallucination and privacy.
- Adoption drivers include time savings and improved decision clarity.

## 7. Market Challenges
- Privacy, compliance, and trust in AI outputs.
- Integration complexity across conferencing and calendar platforms.

## 8. Future Trends
- Multimodal summarization (audio + text + context).
- Embedded AI assistants within collaboration suites.
- Increased demand for on-prem and region-specific data residency.

## 9. Business Opportunity Summary
- Target segments: individuals/freelancers (volume), SMBs/startups (growth), enterprises (high ARPU).
- GTM: early adopters include startups, universities, and consulting firms for pilot programs.

## 10. Target Market Size (high-level table)

| Metric | Estimate |
|---|---:|
| TAM (global) | $6–8B |
| SAM (AI-enhanced notes) | $1.2–2.1B |
| Initial reachable market (Year 1–2) | 10k–100k paying customers |

## 11. Conclusion
- Strong market tailwinds from hybrid work and AI.
- Differentiation via hybrid AI+fallback, enterprise governance, and robust action/decision workflows.
