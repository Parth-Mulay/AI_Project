## Major Decisions
- Anthropic chosen as primary AI provider.
- Deterministic extractive fallback required for reliability and compliance.
- MVP scoped to web SaaS with calendar and communication integrations.
- Prioritize action & decision extraction and admin audit features for enterprise appeal.

## Risks Identified
- Dependency on Anthropic (availability and pricing).
- Potential mistrust in AI outputs (hallucination risk).
- Integration complexity across third-party APIs.
- Data residency and compliance obligations.

## Open Questions
- Target data residency regions for initial enterprise customers?
- Pricing tiers and feature differentiation to optimize conversions?
- Multi-language support at launch or deferred?
- SLA commitments for enterprise customers?

## Assumptions
- Anthropic provides acceptable quality and an SLA.
- Users accept cloud-hosted data with governance controls.
- Extractive fallback can produce acceptable summaries.
