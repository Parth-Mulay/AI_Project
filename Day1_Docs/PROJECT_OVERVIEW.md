# PROJECT OVERVIEW

## 1. Executive Summary

Project: AI Meeting Notes Manager — a SaaS application enabling individuals and teams to create, upload, organize, and enrich meeting notes with AI-powered summaries, action item extraction, decision identification, searchable history, and follow-up management. The platform will use the Anthropic API as the primary generative AI service and include a deterministic extractive NLP fallback to ensure continuity when AI is unavailable.

Primary outcomes:
- Reduce time spent on meeting documentation.
- Improve follow-up completion and decision traceability.
- Provide enterprise-grade governance and compliance features.

Target users: knowledge workers, educators, students, startups, consultants, and enterprise teams.

## 2. Product Vision

Deliver a dependable, privacy-first meeting notes platform that transforms meeting content into actionable knowledge by blending best-in-class generative AI with deterministic fallback summarization.

## 3. Product Mission

Enable individuals and teams to capture meeting knowledge with minimal friction, increase follow-through on decisions and action items, and create an auditable, searchable meeting history while preserving data privacy, transparency, and continuity.

## 4. Business Opportunity

Organizations waste time manually summarizing meetings, leading to missed deadlines and lost institutional knowledge. By automating summarization, extraction of actions/decisions, and centralizing searchable meeting records, the product unlocks measurable productivity gains and recurring subscription revenue.

## 5. Objectives (SMART)
- O1: Achieve 10,000 active users within 12 months after launch.
- O2: Reduce average manual meeting note creation time by 60% for users adopting AI summaries.
- O3: Maintain >= 99.9% system availability for core functions (note creation/search) per month.
- O4: Maintain AI fallback success rate >= 95% when the primary AI is unavailable.
- O5: Reach NPS >= 40 in the first year for paying customers.

## 6. Key Stakeholders
- Product: Product Manager (owner), Business Analyst
- Engineering: Tech Lead, Backend, Frontend, ML engineers
- Design: UX/UI Designer
- Data: Data Engineer, ML Engineer
- Security & Compliance: Security Officer, Legal Counsel
- Sales/Marketing: Growth Lead, Account Managers
- Customers: End users and purchasing decision-makers
- External: Anthropic API, integration partners (calendar, Slack, Zoom, Jira)

## 7. Expected Business Value
- Time savings leading to higher productivity.
- Increased retention via improved team alignment.
- Revenue from tiered subscriptions and enterprise packages.
- Differentiation through a reliable hybrid AI+fallback model.

## 8. Assumptions
- Anthropic API provides required summarization quality and acceptable SLA.
- Users accept cloud-hosted data when proper governance exists.
- Deterministic extractive methods can produce acceptable fallback summaries.
- Integration partners (calendar, conferencing) expose required APIs.

## 9. Constraints
- Dependency on Anthropic for generative summaries.
- Data residency and compliance obligations for enterprise customers.
- Budget and timeline limitations for capstone delivery.
- Performance limits for real-time AI processing at scale.
