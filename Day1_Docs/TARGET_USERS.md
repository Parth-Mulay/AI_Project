# TARGET USERS

For each segment: Goals, Challenges, Expected Benefits, Technology Usage.

## Students
- Goals: Consolidate lecture notes, study efficiently.
- Challenges: Inconsistent notes, time constraints.
- Benefits: Automated summaries, searchable archives.
- Tech: Mobile, Google Workspace, LMS.

## Teachers
- Goals: Preserve learning outcomes, distribute action lists.
- Challenges: Managing many sessions, inconsistent student notes.
- Benefits: Centralized lecture summaries, automating homework extraction.
- Tech: LMS, Zoom.

## Freelancers
- Goals: Maintain client meeting records, create deliverables fast.
- Challenges: Multiclient context switching.
- Benefits: Client-ready summaries, action tracking.
- Tech: Slack, Google Drive.

## Startup Founders
- Goals: Rapid decision-making and documentation.
- Challenges: Limited admin bandwidth.
- Benefits: Decision logs, follow-up tracking.
- Tech: Slack, Notion, Jira.

## Project Managers
- Goals: Convert meeting outcomes into tasks and track progress.
- Challenges: Meeting overload and follow-through.
- Benefits: Action extraction and dashboard.
- Tech: Jira, Trello, Confluence.

## HR Teams
- Goals: Document interviews and 1:1s, enforce retention.
- Challenges: Privacy, compliance.
- Benefits: Audit logs, retention policies.
- Tech: HRIS, Teams.

## Business Teams
- Goals: Reduce meeting overhead, ensure decisions recorded.
- Challenges: Distributed knowledge.
- Benefits: Searchable archive and exports.
- Tech: Google Workspace, Outlook.

## Consultants
- Goals: Produce concise client deliverables.
- Challenges: Multiple client environments.
- Benefits: Polished summaries, templates.
- Tech: Docs, CRMs.

## Researchers
- Goals: Track hypotheses and decisions over time.
- Challenges: Complex discussions and provenance needs.
- Benefits: Tagging and search across meetings.
- Tech: Reference managers, collaborative docs.

(End of Target Users)
